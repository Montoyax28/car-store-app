Why a pre-purchase inspection?

Are you buying a car? - Found the one? Sometimes it might not be what you think is. Let us alleviate the stress of purchasing a new or second-hand vehicle by providing an evaluation of all aspects depending on what level of inspection you choose. Using this, you can re-negotiate a deal and potentially avoid any unwanted consequences of purchasing a used vehicle blindly.

Are you selling your car? - Ready to let go? Conducting a pre-purchase inspection, or should we say pre-selling inspection can assist in selling your car for the right price and price you want. With a positive evaluation, you are able to reassure potential buyers that the vehicle being inspected is in great condition and has been well looked after.

What does a pre-purchase inspection involve? A pre-purchase inspection will be dependant on the vehicle in question though usually including the following:
  • Engine & Transmission (Including/excluding Engine Compression Test)
  • ⇝ Tires
  • ⇝ Wheels & Brakes
  • ⇝ Drivetrain
  • ⇝ Steering & Suspension
  • ⇝ Interior
  • ⇝ Electrical Components
  • ⇝ Undercarriage
  • ⇝ Exterior (Inc. prior accident identification and paint quality)
The vehicle in question may be subject to road testing to check engine performance and all mechanical parts.
Benefits of a pre-purchase evaluation
  • ⇝ Identify potential and current problems: Potential problems are not obvious to the untrained eye. We will evaluate the vehicle in question and report any issues we may discover as well as any concerns that may arise in the future helping you decide whether the vehicle is worth purchasing or not.
  • ⇝ Save money: Purchasing any vehicle with hidden or undisclosed issues will cost you hundreds if not thousands of dollars worth of repairs, knowing what you are purchasing prior to handing your money over will undoubtedly save you money.
  • ⇝ Purchase/Sell with confidence: Purchasing used cars is risky when you don't know what to look out for, hidden defects are a common issue and we're here to help find them. A pre-purchase inspection will give you peace of mind that you are purchasing a vehicle in good condition for a good price in addition to providing your purchaser with the confidence needed as assurance.
  • ⇝ Sell for more: A pre-purchase evaluation from us will give all potential buyers the transparency and confidence that they are purchasing a quality vehicle; even more so you will be in a better position when it comes down to negotiating the final sale price.

About Monster Tunng

  1. Monster Tunng was born in Medellín, Colombia, in 2015, when a group of enthusiasts passionate about the world of motorsports and tuning decided to turn their passion into a business. It all began in a small workshop where vehicles were modified for quarter-mile competitions and custom car shows.

  2. Founder Juan Esteban Montoya, a mechanic and automotive designer with over 10 years of experience, noticed that there weren't enough options in Colombia for those who wanted to transform their cars into true high-performance machines with unique style. Together with his team, he began importing exclusive parts and designing custom kits that quickly gained recognition in the country's tuning community.

  3. Thanks to customer support and the quality of its products, Monster Tunng grew rapidly, transforming from a small workshop into a store specializing in the sale of modified cars, accessories, and tuning parts. Today, the brand has established itself as a leader in the sector, offering high-end products and customization services that take tuning in Colombia to the next level.

  4. With our motto "Turn your car into a beast,", we welcome you to Monster Tunng, where the passion for tuning never stops.
